Thursday, June 16, 2016

2016 Results

Congratulations to all of our gymnasts this year! Great job to Jamarie Saravia for winning 1st place on bars, 2nd place on floor, 3rd place on vault, and 3rd place all-around at the level 5 New York State Championship.
Also, our level 3 gymnast Natalia Higuera won 1st place on floor at the State Championship, and our copper level USAIGC gymnasts Anna Sanscraine won floor, vault, beam and all-around, and Adrianna Li took 3rd all-around.
We look forward to another great year!

Tuesday, June 30, 2015

2015 Results

Nina’s Gymnastics just completed another successful year of gymnastics competition.
We are proud to say that we have two level 10 gymnasts, Leila Gomez and Eden Thomas-Lutchmedial. Congratulations to Eden Thomas-Lutchmedial for qualifying for Level 10 Regionals!
In addition, our level 7 gymnasts are an amazing group. In particular, we have two State Champions. Julia Simon is the 2015 New York State Level 7 state champion on bars and vault and Samantha Lydon is the 2015 New York State Level 7 All-Around State Champion. Both Pamela Amabile and Samantha Lydon were Level 7 Regional Qualifiers and Samantha Lydon is the Level 7 All-Around Regional Champion.
Our level 4 gymnast, Yamilla Campuzano, was the New York State vault champion and Valerie Ryan was the USAIGC New York State vault champion.

Friday, June 27, 2014

2014 Results

After an exciting 2014 competition season we are proud to be the home of the Level 6 NY State Champion Team! In even more exciting news our level 6 girls are ranked #1 in the entire country, beating out hundreds of other schools.

*USA results based on mymeetscores.com

Among this group, Sydney Thomas-Lutchmedial, Pamela Amabile, Samantha Lydon and Brendah Sooter all won 1st place all around and Julia Simon was 2nd place all around in their age division at the New York State Championships! Also worth mentioning is a remarkable 9.9 for Pamela on Uneven Bars!
Eden Thomas-Lutchmedial and our senior gymnast Sophia Wade were level 9 a Regional qualifiers, and Eden competed in level 9 Eastern Nationals!
Our level 3 gymnast Jordan Taylor was 2nd place all around at the New York State Championships! Also level 3, Yamilla Campuzano placed 2nd on Uneven Bars. A great job to all our gymnasts this year!

Sunday, June 2, 2013

2013 Results

Nina's Gymnastics just completed another successful year of gymnastics competitions. In fact,we are proud to say that Nina's Gymnastics is home to four new state champions. Julia Simon is the 2013 New York State level 5 All-Around Champion with a score of 37.4 and great bar performance, taking first place with a score of 9.70.
Sydney Thomas-Lutchmedial is the 2013 New York State level 6 beam champion with an exceptional style scoring 9.45.
Pamela Amabile is the 2013 New York State level 6 vault champion with a very powerful vault scoring 9.45.
In addition to these state champions, this year Nina's has two level 9 Regional Qualifiers, Eden Thomas-Lutchmedial and Sophia Wade. This was another exciting and rewarding gymnastics season. We are proud to recognize the accomplishments and contributions of all our team gymnasts during our 2012-2013 season. Congratulations to each and every one of our gymnasts. We look forward to another exciting and challenging year of competition.
